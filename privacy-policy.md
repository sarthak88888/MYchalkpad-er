# Privacy Policy — MyChalkPad

**Last Updated:** January 1, 2026
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction
MyChalkPad ("we", "our", "us") is a School ERP application. This Privacy
Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use
our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- Phone number (for OTP login and identity verification)
- Name and role (teacher, parent, student, driver, admin, accountant)
- Student information (name, class, section, attendance records)
- Fee payment records
- Attendance data
- Academic marks and grades
- Complaint text and messages

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- Device location (GPS coordinates) — collected ONLY when bus driver
  activates route tracking, for the purpose of showing parents bus location
- Device token for push notifications
- App usage data for crash reporting (via Sentry)

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- Browsing history
- Contacts list
- Photos or files from your device
- Microphone or audio recordings

## 3. How We Use Information
- Authenticate users via Firebase Phone Auth
- Display student attendance, marks, and fee status to parents
- Send SMS alerts to parents about student attendance
- Send WhatsApp messages for school notifications
- Track bus location for parent visibility
- Process fee payments via Razorpay
- Generate academic reports and transfer certificates

## 4. Data Sharing
We share data with:
- **Firebase (Google):** For authentication and data storage
- **Fast2SMS:** For SMS notifications to parents
- **Interakt:** For WhatsApp notifications
- **Razorpay:** For fee payment processing
- **Sentry:** For crash reporting (anonymized error data only)

We do NOT sell your data to any third party.

## 5. Data Storage and Security
- All data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore
- Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- Access is restricted by Firebase Security Rules
- Each user can only access data relevant to their role
- Phone authentication ensures only verified users can log in

## 6. Location Data
- GPS location is collected ONLY from bus drivers
- Location is collected ONLY when the driver activates a route
- Location data is visible only to parents of students on that bus route
- Location data is NOT stored permanently — it is overwritten every 30 seconds
- Location collection stops immediately when driver stops the route

## 7. Children's Privacy
- Our app is used in school environments and may process student data
- Student data is provided by school administrators
- We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children
- Schools are responsible for obtaining necessary parental consent

## 8. Data Retention
- User accounts are retained as long as the school uses the service
- Attendance and marks data is retained for the academic year
- Fee payment records are retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
- SMS logs are retained for 90 days

## 9. Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Request access to your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Withdraw consent for notifications

Contact your school administrator to exercise these rights.
